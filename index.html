<faq>

Common questions

What does ByteDX do?+

We build software for education, engineering, and data science. We research a problem on the ground first, then build a focused product around it. Our first product is EduSphere, a school management system.

What is EduSphere?+

EduSphere is a digital school management system that replaces manual attendance registers, paper fee cards, and WhatsApp-based parent updates with one dashboard for attendance, students, teachers, and fees.

Does ByteDX only work with schools?+

No. Education is our first market, but we take on engineering, database, and digital transformation work for other businesses as well.

How much does EduSphere cost?+

Pricing is a one-time installation fee plus a flat monthly subscription, based on school size. Get in touch below for a quote specific to your school.

Where is ByteDX based?+

Gujranwala, Pakistan. We work directly with the schools and businesses we serve.
